For Shops & Training Centers

The evaluator model brings certification directly to the bench.

🏭 Evaluator Model

Qualified evaluators at your shop can sign off on practical tasks as technicians complete them on real aircraft, in real time.

  • No travel required for task completion
  • Tasks signed off on real work, not simulations
  • Evaluators trained and certified by AEA

📋 Train-the-Trainer

AEA certifies senior technicians as evaluators through a structured train-the-trainer program, building capacity at every participating shop.

  • Evaluator certification pathway
  • Quality metrics tracked per evaluator
  • Consistent standards across all shops

📈 Progress Visibility

Shop managers and AEA staff can see exactly where every technician stands — no guessing, no spreadsheets, no phone calls.

  • Real-time pipeline dashboard
  • Section-by-section progress tracking
  • Automated readiness notifications
